Weather Analysis

This project displays the results of a climate analysis on cities around the world. Over 500 cities were randomly selected for the analysis.

The weather conditions (temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity) were measured for every city in the data set. Latitude was used to look for weather paterns in relationship to city distance from the equator. Visualizations for each weather condition vs city latitude can be founb on this site. Ultimately, the only strong correlation existed between latitude and temperature. As cities get closer to the equator, they have a warmer average temperature.
